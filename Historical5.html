The Future of Mammoth Cave

About

New passages and corridors are being discovered at Mammoth Cave National Park every day. The “New Discovery” area in Mammoth Cave is one of such sites. Nobody since the Native Americans have been in most of the passages in that area.

Mapping new areas isn’t easy: cavers are underground for 30 hours that include 1,000-foot belly crawls and technical rope climbing.

Some cavers were lost for several hours. No caver has been seriously hurt, however there is no immediate rescue for an injured caver.

Conclusion

Mammoth Cave has a long history, from the native Americans to the War of 1812. When I was learning about the War of 1812, I had no idea that Mammoth Cave made gunpowder for the United States!

I think that Mammoth Cave is one of the most interesting national parks in the United States. By now, if you don’t know everything, please message me with the contact information on the home page.
